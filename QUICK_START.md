## Daily Use

### How to Process Invoices

1. **Double-click "Accudent Importer"** on your Desktop
2. **Drag and drop** your `.pages` invoice files into the window
3. A popup will ask you to **confirm the dentist name** - edit if needed
4. Click **"Confirm"** 
5. Done! Files are saved to `~/Documents/AccudentLab/YYYY-MM/`

### What You Get

For each month, the app creates:
- üìä **Excel file** - Spreadsheet with all invoices
- üìÑ **PDF Report** - Professional statement with your logo
- üìù **CSV file** - Data backup

### Finding Your Files

The app automatically opens the Excel and PDF files when done. 

You can also find them:
1. Open **Finder**
2. Go to **Documents ‚Üí AccudentLab**
3. Each month has its own folder (like `2025-10`)
